A New Approach to Network Visualization

CenturyLink Technology Solutions (CTS) is a major player in the Data Centre space, focused on managed hosting and co-location services.

They’re positioned at the top of their field because they have everything you’d expect from a successful company – great products, excellent customer service, competitive pricing and a helpful and effective workforce. CenturyLink Technology Solutions also focuses on consistent innovation to ensure they continue offering best-in-class solutions to their market.

Even with all of these positive characteristics, CTS still has to compete with big-name competitors like Amazon and Rackspace. And with the rise of cloud computing, they also face a seismic shift in the way their industry does business.

CTS wants to continue leading the way by approaching their offering differently, showing their market they have fresh, exciting technology and are going to continue being a top company in their field – now and into the future.

The Challenge

Traditionally, companies had to buy physical hardware to create a private data centre network. It was extremely costly and demanded a great deal of time for company specialists to build and deploy. In some cases, technicians had to physically travel to the hosting location, often very far away, to set up and configure hardware manually.
